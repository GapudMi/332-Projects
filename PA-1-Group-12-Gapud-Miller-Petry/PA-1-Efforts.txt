How to run the program?
After opening the PA-1-Executable.exe program, the program will automatically create the four results and statistics spreadsheets in the same file as the executable.
The program will display the amount of time saved between algorithms as in the Conclusions.txt file.
When you are finished with the program, you can press enter as requested, and it will close.

How were spreadsheets generated?
By the program.
